Tips for a better mental health

Regular physical activity increases the production of endorphins, which are natural mood lifters and reduces stress hormones, such as adrenaline and cortisol.

A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains can improve overall brain function.

Quality sleep is essential for emotional regulation and cognitive function. Poor sleep is linked to mental health conditions such as depression and anxiety.

Social support is crucial for mental health. Having strong, supportive relationships can improve your ability to cope with stress and provide a sense of belonging and purpose .

Mindfulness practices, such as meditation, have been shown to reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression by promoting relaxation and enhancing the ability to manage stress .
